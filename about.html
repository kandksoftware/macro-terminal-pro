About us

We develop specialized CNC software solutions that unlock the full capabilities of modern CNC controls. Our focus is on creating custom macro systems and automation tools for Fanuc, Haas, and other controls that support Fanuc Macro B parametric programming. While many machines already include advanced macro functionality, it is rarely used to its full potential. Our software turns these built-in capabilities into powerful production tools.
Our experience in CNC machining and manufacturing automation allows us to design solutions that directly address real shop-floor challenges. By collaborating with machinists, CNC programmers, and manufacturing engineers, we develop macro systems that integrate smoothly with existing workflows and machining strategies. The result is improved efficiency, reduced setup times, and greater process consistency across multiple machines.
Fanuc Macro B programming is at the core of our work. This parametric language enables intelligent machining operations, automated routines, and flexible programming logic. By combining CNC software development with deep machining expertise, we create scalable automation solutions that simplify programming and significantly increase manufacturing productivity.
Our CNC Software Solutions Include:
  • Custom Fanuc Macro B programming
  • CNC automation software for Fanuc and Haas controls
  • Parametric machining cycles
  • Automated probing and setup routines
  • CNC tool management and tool life macros
  • Production optimization and process standardization
  • Custom macro libraries for CNC machines

Precision machining deserves precision software.
