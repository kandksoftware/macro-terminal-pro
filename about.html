About us

We develop advanced CNC software solutions designed to maximize the capabilities of modern machine tools. Our company specializes in creating custom software and macro systems for Fanuc, Haas, and other CNC controls that support Fanuc Macro B parametric programming.
With a strong background in CNC machining and automation, we help manufacturers improve productivity, reduce setup time, and standardize machining processes across their shop floor. Our solutions are built specifically for real production environments where reliability, efficiency, and repeatability are critical.
Our core expertise is Fanuc Macro B programming, a powerful parametric language that allows CNC machines to perform intelligent operations, automate repetitive tasks, and simplify complex machining workflows. By combining macro programming with deep machining knowledge, we create flexible software systems that adapt to the needs of modern manufacturing.
Our CNC Software Solutions Include:
  • Custom Fanuc Macro B programming
  • CNC automation software for Fanuc and Haas controls
  • Parametric machining cycles
  • Automated probing and setup routines
  • CNC tool management and tool life macros
  • Production optimization and process standardization
  • Custom macro libraries for CNC machines
Our software helps machine shops, manufacturers, and production facilities unlock the full power of their CNC controls. Many machines already support advanced macro capabilities, but these features are often underutilized. Our solutions turn those capabilities into practical tools that improve efficiency, accuracy, and consistency in daily machining operations.
We work closely with machinists, programmers, and manufacturing engineers to develop CNC macro systems that integrate seamlessly into existing workflows. Whether you operate a small machine shop or a large production facility, our goal is to deliver scalable CNC software that improves performance across every machine.
By combining CNC software development, Fanuc Macro B expertise, and real-world machining knowledge, we help manufacturers automate processes, reduce programming complexity, and achieve higher productivity from their CNC equipment.
If you're looking for custom CNC macros, Fanuc Macro B solutions, or automation software for Fanuc and Haas CNC machines, our team is ready to help you transform the way your machines operate.

Precision machining deserves precision software.
