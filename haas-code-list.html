The company produces a full range of CNC mills, CNC lathes, rotary tables, and integrated CNC control systems used in machine shops, aerospace manufacturing, automotive production, and engineering workshops worldwide. Haas machines are known for their reliability, accessible controls, and strong presence in CNC machining and programming environments.
